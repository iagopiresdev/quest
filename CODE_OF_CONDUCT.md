# Code of Conduct

## Our Standard

Contributors are expected to keep discussion direct, respectful, and technical.
Disagreement is normal. Personal attacks, harassment, and disruptive behavior are not.

## Expected Behavior

- critique code and decisions, not people
- keep claims concrete and reproducible
- respect project scope and review boundaries
- avoid posting secrets or private operational data

## Unacceptable Behavior

- harassment or discrimination
- doxxing or disclosure of private information
- bad-faith technical derailment
- spam, abuse, or intimidation

## Enforcement

Maintainers may remove comments, reject contributions, or ban participants whose behavior makes the
project harder to review, maintain, or use safely.
